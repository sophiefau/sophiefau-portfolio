      About

      Originally from France, I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Graphic Design from Université Laval in Canada, where I developed skills in graphic design, web design, and UI/UX design. After working for two years as a graphic designer in an agency and as a freelancer, I moved to Berlin in Germany and gained experience in customer service. Among these roles, I worked for Facebook's advertising services, building expertise in e-commerce and online marketing.

      Recently, I completed an intensive 6-month Full-Stack Web Development Bootcamp at CareerFoundry, where I honed my technical skills to complement my design background. This specific combination enables me to create websites and applications that are functional, visually engaging and user-focused.
